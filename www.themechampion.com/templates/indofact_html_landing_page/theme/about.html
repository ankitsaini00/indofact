Company

Durare Shield is a manufacturing firm that makes specialized cladding that are used across industries for various purposes including protection from chemical fumes, rust protection, insulation, temperature control and cleaning & maintenance.

Our foundation dates back to the year 1986 when the company was established as “Raj Chemicals”. In 2020, we rebranded and restructured ourselves into “Durare Shield” to better align with the market and refine our strategies.

We have conducted our original research and development and strenuously strived for the innovation of our line of products. These efforts have allowed us to create specialty chemicals, coatings, rheological additives, and anti-corrosion systems.

Our specialty range includes anti-corrosive cladding system, chemical resistant cladding system, high-end infrastructural coatings.

HISTORY & LEGACY

Established in 1986, our primary goal has always been to explore the possibilities in science and technology to create specific solution-providing products. We started with manufacturing of additives, lacquers, ceramic binders, surfactants etc. Research and development has been at the core of Durare Shield since the initiation and we have travelled along all these years shaving it as the defining base for each product we launch. We started as a small scale company in Vallabh Vidyanagar, which steadily scaled to encompass mass production of our specialty products with due diligence. Our current facility consists of research laboratories, fully-equipped manufacturing units, testing units and transport decks.

The founder, Mr Rajesh Shah, has the vision to create the best possible line of products that targets specific, long-term solutions that are durable, high-quality and sustainable. His vision has unfolded into the range of products we offer.

Mission

To give an ultimate solution for all types of shields for pipes, tanks, roofs, equipment which gives a long lasting life of the base material.

Vision

To create the best possible line of products that targets specific, long-term solutions that are durable, high-quality and sustainable.
