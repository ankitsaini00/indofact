Hardshield is a special high durability paint and coating system and technologically advanced solution that shields your walls forever.

Water-based specialized paint systems with superior strength and durability for industrial and commercial use, for use in extreme weather condition regions, historical monuments (clear coat), special residencies and any wall you wish to beautify and preserve for a long time.

Solutions: Blistering, cracking or flaking, efflorescence, mildew, stain resistance, peeling, water leaching clouding of film, and poor hiding. Provides weather resistance, strength and durability to walls of: chemical plants, pharmaceutical plants, water treatment plants, High humid & windy regions, Cemented and concrete surfaces, or any other walls.
